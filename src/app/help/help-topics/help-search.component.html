How to Search

There are two primary types of searches in the Digital Archive:

  • 1000s of "HistoryMakers", i.e., the people that were interviewed in the archive. From the Maker Directory page, you will see a set of HistoryMakers. You can issue a search to the biographies and see a resulting set of people. You can use advanced biography-search capabilities, such as whether to search on just the name.
  • 100,000s of Stories, i.e., what these people spoke about. From the Home page, you can issue a search and see a resulting set of stories. It has its own advanced story-search capability as well. Typical searches work against the story transcript (spoken words plus any transcription notes) and story title (created by people to represent the story).

In addition, there is a Topic Search page where stories that have been manually tagged into topics can be retrieved. Currently, {{tagMatchDetails}} Topic Search page.
